Discover New Music

Here you can find a collection of songs, from the past and present which have been influencing sounds I have been creating. Hopefully you come accross something you enjoy!

Umi Says - Mos Def

A classic song by Mos Def, this song tells the tale of a man who is trying to do his best, whilst looking for the meaning of freedom. This one is certain to make you think.

Happy - Flava D

One of my favourite tracks, flava d couldnt have done much more to make this song any better. If you're a fan of garage this s a must listen!

Make Change - Goldies Band

I was up late one night when i was younger and I found myself watching the documentary following goldie as he formed a band of people from underprivaledged backgrounds with exceptional talent.

Cherish the day - Robert Glasper Experiment ft. Lalah Hathaway

Incredibly soulful song, the live version is just as good as the recorded studio, but I always feel it's best to the talent of artists in a live environment

Reflecting - Skepta

I had to include this song, Skepta at his best. Here is an example of an artist who kept doing his thing and hard work has paid off.

Come Close J Dilla Remix - Common ft Pharrell, Q Tip & Erykah Badu

The artists on this feature speak for themselves.
